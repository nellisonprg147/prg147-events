Sparta Elroy And The Four Trails Across Wisconsin

Every year Brian, Jodey, and myself go one a trip. This was the 4 Trails and Sparta Elory trail
that runs across the middle region of Wisconsin. The trip was 210 miles in three days.
We carried all of our gear. Ride, camp, then ride again was the theme of our adventure.
